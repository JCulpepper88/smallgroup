As a prisoner for the Lord, then, I urge you to live a life worthy of the calling you have received. Be completely

through the bond of peace. There is one body and one Spirit, just as you were called to one hope when you were

- Ephesians 4:1-6

Welcome to the men's bible study group from Church of the Highlands. This group meets in the Hoover area of Alabama. In this group, we study books of the bible, topics of the bible, or go through bible studies from books or videos. This group is about more than bible study, however. We build relationships with one another, support each other during trials in life and celebrate all the good things!

On this website, you will find several tools to help you interact with the small group.

  • On the Prayer Requests page, each week's prayer requests will be listed for reference. In addition, each member will be able to add prayer requests from a computer. The list is only viewable from a mobile device. However, to keep these private, a password is required to view the list. This password will be given out in the group meetings.

  • Moving to the Study Topics page, you will find previous and current studies the group has gone through. Since this website is beginning in 2018, that is as far back as you will be able to see.

  • The Calendar page links to a Google calendar which is set up for the small group. It will reflect the meeting schedule for the group, any weeks we may be skipping, as well as any other extra events (going out to eat, a birthday gathering, etc.) we may decide we want to do.

  • Announcements will be anything we want to communicate to the group. Most of this should go out through text messages, but it will be kept here for reference. Non-urgent messages, such as information about previous study material, will not be texted out. Therefore, new information will show up on this page occasionally.

  • On the Contact Info page will be listed the names, pictures, phone numbers and email addresses of the group leaders.

  • The Resources page is a list of links to outside resources the group leaders feel are of value to group members. This list will likely change over time, so please alert one of the group leaders if you have a recommendation for a good resource.
